Here are some instructions for you

Here's how you authenticate yourself

        http://[username]:[password]@dyndns.hipernet.hu

You just call /update with your parameters

        /update?hostname=[fullname]&myip=[IP]

Where fullname may look like example.dyndns.hipernet.hu

And IP may look something like 1.2.3.4 or you may leave it empty to use your current IP.
